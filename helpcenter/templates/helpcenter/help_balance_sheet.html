The Balance Sheet shows your company’s financial position at a point in time.

Sections

  • Assets: cash, accounts receivable, etc.
  • Liabilities: accounts payable, taxes payable, etc.
  • Equity: retained earnings / owner’s equity.

Core invariant

Assets = Liabilities + Equity
If this does not hold, posting or account setup is broken.
