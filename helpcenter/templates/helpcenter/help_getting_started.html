{% extends "helpcenter/_help_base.html" %} {% block help_h1 %}Getting started{% endblock %} {% block help_body %}
  1. Create/select a company using the company switcher in the sidebar.
  2. Invite team members (Company settings → Team). Assign roles based on responsibilities.
  3. Create clients (Clients → New) and add contact details.
  4. Create projects and assign staff. Project visibility governs what Staff can see.
  5. Track time (Time tracking) and use approval/billing states to control the workflow.
  6. Create documents (Invoices/Estimates/Proposals). Convert unbilled time into invoices where applicable.
  7. Record payments including partial payments and overpayments (credits are tracked).
  8. Track expenses and upload receipts.
Tip
If you get blocked by subscription gates, check Billing → Overview and confirm trial/plan status.
