Statements summarize what a client owes across multiple invoices. They are most useful when a client has multiple open items or pays on a schedule.

When to use statements

  • Monthly billing cycles (send at the start/end of month).
  • Clients who pay by check/ACH and prefer a single “what’s due” summary.
  • High-volume service work where individual invoices may be missed.

Operational checklist

  • Confirm invoices are Sent (not Draft).
  • Confirm any credits have been applied correctly.
  • Confirm payment links / instructions match your business process.
