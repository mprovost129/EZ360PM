We take security seriously. This page describes security safeguards we use to help protect Personal Information and User Content, as well as steps you should take to keep your account secure.

Security Safeguards

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect Personal Information and User Content against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. These measures may include access controls, encryption in transit, monitoring, logging, and least-privilege internal access.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for using strong passwords, keeping credentials confidential, and promptly notifying us of suspected unauthorized access.

Account Security

  • You are responsible for all activity under your account.
  • Keep your credentials confidential and use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) where available or required by your organization.
  • If you believe your account has been compromised, notify us immediately.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact support@ez360pm.com. We may require credential resets and may temporarily suspend access to protect you and the platform.
