Getting started

  1. Create a project: Choose Hourly or Flat rate, set a budget, define start/due dates, and assign team members.
  2. Track progress: Use timers or weekly logs to record hours. Monitor budget vs. actuals on the project overview page.
  3. Bill work: Add unbilled time and expenses to invoices with one click, keeping billing in sync with your projects.

Tips for organization

Tip: Use project numbers and statuses (Active, On hold, Done) to keep your project list tidy and searchable.
