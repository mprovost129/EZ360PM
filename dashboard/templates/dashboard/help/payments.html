How to manage payments

  1. Record a payment: Open an invoice → Add Payment. Choose the method, date, and amount received.
  2. Enable online payments: Connect Stripe in Billing to let clients pay instantly by card or digital wallet.
  3. Reconcile balances: Confirm paid invoices and follow up on any overdue amounts from the Payments page.
Tip: Set up automatic reminders (“dunning”) in invoice settings to reduce days sales outstanding (DSO) and speed up collection.
