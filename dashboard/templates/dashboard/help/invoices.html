Invoices

Learn how to create and manage invoices in {{ APP_NAME }}. You can save drafts, reuse templates, and send invoices directly to clients.

  1. Create an invoice: Go to the Invoices section and click New Invoice. Fill in client details, line items, and due dates.
  2. Save as a template: If you often reuse the same format, you can save an invoice as a template for quick creation in the future.
  3. Send an invoice: Once finalized, send the invoice via email or share the secure link. Clients can pay online if payments are enabled.
Tip: You can track whether invoices are viewed and paid. Automated reminders help you follow up with clients faster.
