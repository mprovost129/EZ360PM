Time Tracking

Track your work hours in {{ APP_NAME }} with timers or manual logs. You can manage time directly from projects and roll it into invoices when ready.

  1. Start a timer: Open a project and click Start Timer. The timer runs until you stop it.
  2. Stop a timer: When you finish working, click Stop. The time entry is saved automatically.
  3. Log time manually: From the Time section, add hours and notes without using a timer.
Tip: View all logged hours under Time > Entries. Unbilled time can be added to invoices with a single click.
