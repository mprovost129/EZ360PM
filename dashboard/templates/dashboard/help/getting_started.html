Getting Started

Set up {{ APP_NAME }} in just a few steps. Once your company, clients, and first project are ready, you can begin tracking time and sending invoices.

  1. Create your company: Add your company name, contact details, and upload a logo.
  2. Add a client and project: Enter client details, then create a project (hourly or flat rate).
  3. Track and invoice: Start a timer or log time manually. Unbilled hours can be added to invoices and sent to your client.
