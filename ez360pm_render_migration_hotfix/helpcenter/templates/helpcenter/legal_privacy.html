This policy describes how EZ360PM collects, uses, and protects personal data.

Information we collect

  • Account information (name, email, username).
  • Company/business information you provide.
  • Usage and security data (e.g., audit logs, login events, operational telemetry).
  • Files you upload (receipts, project files) stored in private object storage.

How we use information

  • To provide the Service, authenticate users, and enforce permissions.
  • To process billing via Stripe.
  • To prevent fraud and improve reliability (Ops, monitoring, audits).

Data sharing

  • Stripe (billing processing).
  • AWS/S3 (file storage) when enabled.
  • Service providers for monitoring/logging if configured.

Data retention

Retention policies are documented in Ops and may be configured per environment.

Your choices

  • Access and update account/profile information.
  • Owners/Admins control company membership and access to company data.
