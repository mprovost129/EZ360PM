Ops is the launch hardening surface. It is restricted to staff/admins (depending on your internal policy).

Key pages
  • Ops Dashboard — health, versions, and operational status.
  • Launch Checks — Launch Gate items that must be green to go live.
  • Storage — S3 posture, smoke tests, production readiness checklist.
  • Retention — data retention policy and cleanup operations.
  • Support Mode — staff-only temporary access to a customer company for support.
Launch Gate
Treat Launch Gate as a checklist with evidence. Don’t ship until every required item is complete and dated.
