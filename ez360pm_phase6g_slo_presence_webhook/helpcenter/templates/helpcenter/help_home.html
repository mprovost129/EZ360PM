Recommended launch flow
  1. Create company → invite team → assign roles.
  2. Create client → create project → assign staff.
  3. Track time (draft → submit → approve) and convert unbilled to invoice.
  4. Record payment (partial supported) and confirm invoice status updates.
  5. Add expenses with receipts (direct upload) and confirm private download works.
  6. Upload project files (direct upload) and confirm private download works.
  7. Ops → Launch Checks: confirm all gates green before going live.
